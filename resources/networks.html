Finding Direct Programs

How to Find Affiliate Programs

1. Check Website Footers: Look for "Affiliates," "Partners," or "Earn With Us" links at the bottom of any product website.

2. Google Search: Search "[Brand Name] affiliate program" or "[Product] partner program"

3. Browse Network Directories: Log into ShareASale, Impact, or CJ and browse by category

4. Ask Directly: Email brands without public programs - many have private affiliate arrangements

5. Study Competitors: Check what programs successful sites in your niche promote
