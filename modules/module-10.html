📖 Analytics Setup & Understanding

Why Tracking Matters

Without tracking, you're flying blind. Proper analytics help you answer critical questions:

  • ✓
    Which content pieces are actually generating revenue?
  • ✓
    Where is your traffic coming from?
  • ✓
    Which products convert best for your audience?
  • ✓
    What should you create more of (and less of)?

Google Analytics 4 Setup Guide

Step 1

Create GA4 Property

Go to analytics.google.com, sign in with Google account, and create a new GA4 property for your website.

Step 2

Install Tracking Code

Add the GA4 tracking snippet to your site. For WordPress, use a plugin like "Site Kit by Google" or add to your theme's header.

Step 3

Set Up Events for Affiliate Links

Track outbound clicks to affiliate links as events. Use Google Tag Manager or configure click tracking in GA4 to monitor affiliate link clicks.

Step 4

Connect Search Console

Link Google Search Console to GA4 to see which search queries bring traffic and track SEO performance.

Key Metrics for Affiliate Marketers

Traffic Metrics

  • Sessions: Total visits to your site
  • Users: Unique visitors
  • Traffic Sources: Where visitors come from
  • Top Pages: Most visited content

Engagement Metrics

  • Avg. Time on Page: How long people read
  • Bounce Rate: Single-page visits
  • Scroll Depth: How far people scroll
  • Pages/Session: Content engagement

Affiliate Metrics

  • Click-Through Rate: Clicks / Pageviews
  • Conversion Rate: Sales / Clicks
  • EPC: Earnings Per 100 Clicks
  • Revenue Per Visitor: Total rev / Total visits

Network Dashboard Metrics

  • Clicks: Total affiliate link clicks
  • Conversions: Completed sales/actions
  • Pending/Confirmed: Commission status
  • Reversal Rate: Returned orders

Network Dashboard Navigation

Each affiliate network has its own dashboard. Key sections to monitor:

Performance Reports

View clicks, conversions, and revenue by date range, merchant, or link. Export data for deeper analysis.

Link Management

Access your affiliate links, create new tracking links, and manage link parameters for campaign tracking.

Payment History

Track pending and paid commissions. Understand payment schedules and thresholds.

Merchant Communication

Message merchants, access promotional materials, and stay updated on program changes.

Data-Driven Optimization

Monthly Review Process

1. Top Performers: Identify your top 5 revenue-generating pages. What do they have in common? Create more like them.

2. Underperformers: Find high-traffic pages with low conversions. Optimize CTAs, link placement, or try different products.

3. Traffic Trends: Which sources are growing? Double down on what's working.

4. Product Performance: Are certain products converting better? Promote winners more prominently.

5. Seasonal Patterns: Note seasonal trends to plan future content.
