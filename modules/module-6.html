📖 High-Converting Content Types

The Buyer's Journey & Content Match

Different content types serve different stages of the buyer's journey. Understanding this helps you create the right content:

1

Awareness

"I have a problem..."

Content Types:
  • Educational blog posts
  • "How to" guides
  • Problem-focused videos

Affiliate potential: Low (informational intent)

→
2

Consideration

"What are my options?"

Content Types:
  • "Best [Product] for [Use Case]"
  • Product comparisons
  • Alternatives to [Brand]

Affiliate potential: High (commercial intent)

→
3

Decision

"Should I buy this?"

Content Types:
  • In-depth product reviews
  • "[Product] Review: Honest Take"
  • Video demonstrations

Affiliate potential: Highest (purchase intent)

The 80/20 Rule for Affiliate Content

Spend 80% of your effort on consideration and decision stage content - this is where the money is. Informational content builds authority but converts poorly. Prioritize "Best X," reviews, and comparisons for your affiliate site.

Content Type #1: Product Reviews

The backbone of affiliate marketing. A great review helps someone decide if a product is right for them.

Product Review Structure

1. Hook/Introduction

State who this review is for and your bottom-line verdict upfront. Don't make readers scroll to find your recommendation.

2. Quick Summary Box

Product specs, rating, price, and key pros/cons at a glance. Cater to skimmers.

3. Who Is This For?

Clearly define the ideal user. This builds trust - you're not just pushing products on everyone.

4. Features Deep Dive

Walk through key features with your experience. Include screenshots, photos, or video clips.

5. Pros & Cons (Honest)

List real advantages and genuine drawbacks. Mentioning negatives increases credibility.

6. Pricing & Value

Discuss price, any deals, and whether it's worth the investment for different users.

7. Alternatives

Briefly mention 2-3 alternatives for readers who may need something different.

8. Final Verdict & CTA

Summarize who should buy, who shouldn't, and include your affiliate link with a clear call-to-action.

9. FAQ Section

Answer common questions. This also helps with SEO featured snippets.

Content Type #2: Comparison Posts

Comparisons target people deciding between specific products - extremely high purchase intent.

A vs B Comparison

"Notion vs Obsidian: Which Note-Taking App is Right for You?"

Best when two products are commonly compared. High search volume for "[Product A] vs [Product B]"

Alternatives Posts

"7 Best Photoshop Alternatives (Free & Paid Options)"

Targets people unhappy with a popular product or seeking cheaper options.

Best-Of Roundups

"Best Project Management Software for Small Teams in 2025"

Comprehensive guides that compare multiple products for a specific use case.

Content Type #3: "Best Of" Roundup Posts

These comprehensive guides compare multiple products and are SEO goldmines.

Why "Best Of" Posts Work

SEO Value: "Best [product type] for [use case]" keywords have high search volume and clear commercial intent.

Multiple Opportunities: You can include 5-15 affiliate links in one post, each to different products.

Authority Building: Comprehensive guides position you as an expert who has tested everything.

Evergreen Traffic: With annual updates, these posts can drive traffic for years.

Using AI for Content Creation

In 2025, AI is a force multiplier for affiliate content. Here's how to use it effectively:

💡

Brainstorming & Research

Ask AI: "What questions do people have about [Product X]?" or "What are the top concerns when buying [Product Category]?"

📄

Outline Generation

Feed AI your template and ask it to create an outline for your specific product. Review and adjust based on your knowledge.

✏

First Draft

Use AI to draft sections, but always add your personal experience, opinions, and unique insights. AI writes the bones; you add the soul.

🔍

Fact-Checking

Verify all specifications, prices, and claims. AI can hallucinate details. Your credibility depends on accuracy.

🎨

Personalization

Add your voice, real experiences, screenshots/photos, and genuine opinions. This is what makes your content unique and trustworthy.

AI Content Warning

Never publish AI content without:
1. Adding your personal experience and opinions
2. Verifying all facts and specifications
3. Including original images/screenshots
4. Editing for your unique voice

Google penalizes thin, unhelpful AI content. Your audience can tell the difference. AI should assist, not replace, your expertise.
